### 3. FERPA Compliance Agreement

Employee understands and agrees to comply with FERPA requirements, including:

**a) Legitimate Educational Interest**
- Employee will access student education records ONLY when required for legitimate job duties
- Employee will NOT access student records out of curiosity or for personal reasons

**b) Prohibition on Unauthorized Disclosure**
- Employee will NOT disclose student PII to unauthorized individuals
- Employee will NOT discuss student information in public areas or with family/friends
- Employee will NOT share login credentials or allow others to access systems using Employee's account

**c) Proper Handling of Student Data**
- Employee will follow company policies for data handling, storage, and transmission
- Employee will use only approved systems and methods for accessing student data
- Employee will NOT download student data to personal devices without authorization
- Employee will NOT email student PII without encryption or proper safeguards

**d) Reporting Obligations**
- Employee will immediately report any suspected FERPA violations
- Employee will immediately report any suspected data breaches or security incidents
- Employee will immediately report lost or stolen devices containing student data

### 4. Confidentiality Obligations

Employee agrees to maintain strict confidentiality of:

- **Student Education Records:** All academic records, transcripts, grades, enrollment data
- **Personally Identifiable Information:** Names, email addresses, student IDs, demographic data
- **University Partner Information:** University data, agreements, and business relationships
- **Company Proprietary Information:** Business strategies, security procedures, technical implementations

### 5. Access Level Acknowledgment

Employee's approved access tier: [ ] Tier 0  [ ] Tier 1  [ ] Tier 2  [ ] Tier 3  [ ] Tier 4

Employee understands that:
- Access is granted based on job role and necessity
- Access will be monitored and logged
- Unauthorized access attempts will result in disciplinary action
- Access will be revoked immediately upon termination of employment

### 6. Duration of Obligations

These confidentiality obligations:
- Begin on the date of signing
- Continue throughout employment
- **Survive termination of employment indefinitely**

Employee's obligations under FERPA and this agreement continue AFTER employment ends.

### 7. Consequences of Violation

Employee understands that violation of this agreement may result in:

**For Employee:**
- Disciplinary action up to and including immediate termination
- Personal legal liability
- Criminal prosecution (in cases of identity theft or fraud)
- Civil penalties

**For Universities:**
- Loss of federal funding
- Reputational harm
- Legal liability

### 8. Return of Data

Upon termination of employment, Employee agrees to:
- Return all company devices and materials
- Delete all company data from personal devices
- NOT retain copies of student data or confidential information
- NOT use student data or company information in future employment

By signing below, Employee acknowledges that:

- I have read and understand this agreement
- I have completed required FERPA training
- I understand FERPA requirements and my obligations
- I agree to comply with all terms of this agreement
- I understand the consequences of violating this agreement
- I will immediately report any violations or security incidents
